Our Founder

Hi, I’m Millie

I created florence by mills because I believe beauty should be fun, effortless, and above all — kind. Every product is vegan, cruelty-free, and made with clean ingredients that actually work.

Named after my great-grandmother Florence, this brand is all about embracing who you are. No filters, no rules — just you, glowing.
